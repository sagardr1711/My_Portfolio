About me

A Little About Me, A Lot About Passion

I’m not just a creator—I’m a curious mind, a storyteller, and someone who sees beauty in the details most people overlook. Whether I’m behind a camera, designing a UI, or writing a script, my work is driven by one question: “What story needs to be told?” With a background in Computer Science and Psychology, I blend logic with empathy, design with depth, and visuals with voice. My creative journey started in unexpected places—editing short videos in my room, capturing forgotten traditions like Theyyam, and experimenting with design that feels as intuitive as it looks. Over time, what began as curiosity evolved into craft. I'm self-taught in tools like DaVinci Resolve, Adobe Premiere Pro, and Figma, constantly exploring the intersection of technology, storytelling, and emotion. I don’t claim to be a master—but I’m a relentless learner who believes growth is the real flex. Outside my creative bubble, I’ve had the honor of serving as the President of CAVT and a Student Council Member, where leadership became another lens through which I understand people and perspectives. In a world full of noise, I aim to create something that feels. Whether it’s a documentary that moves you, a design that speaks to you, or an idea that makes you pause—I want my work to do more than impress. I want it to connect. Welcome to my world. Let’s create something meaningful.
